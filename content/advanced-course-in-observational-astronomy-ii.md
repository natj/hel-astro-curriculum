---
title: Advanced Course in Observational Astronomy II (PAP307)
---

Advanced astronomical data analysis using ESO archives, pipelines, and spectroscopic data.

- **Period**: 4
- **Content**: 24 lectures, intense week in Turku, 4 assignments
- **Themes**: Not specified
- **Teachers**: [[Alexis Finoguenov]]

---
## Content

- ESO instrumentation on Paranal and future developments on Armazones
- ALMA: telescopes, operations, data reduction
- 4MOST/VISTA facility and observing tools
- VLT data analysis using selected instruments: KMOS, MUSE, UVES, XSHOOTER, SoX
- ESO proposal writing
- VST and data analysis
- JWST and data analysis
- Euclid and its data repository
- Data visualization tools
- ESO data archive
