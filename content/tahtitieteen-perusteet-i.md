Overview of astrophysics and space physics research, key concepts, coordinate systems, and radiation mechanisms.

- **Period**: 3
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises
- **Themes**: Theory 25%, Methods 25%, Observations 20%, Phenomena 30%
- **Teachers**: [[Mika Juvela]]

---
## Content

- Coordinate systems, calendars, and time measurement
- Telescopes and observations (optical, high-energy, radio, particle)
- Radiation quantities (intensity, flux density, luminosity, magnitudes, colours, extinction)
- Radiation mechanisms (free-free, synchrotron, and black body; radiative transfer equation)
- Celestial mechanics (Kepler’s laws, orbital elements, two-body systems)
- Solar system physics (objects, albedos, temperature estimates, exoplanet detection)
