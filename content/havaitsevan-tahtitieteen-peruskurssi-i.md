Introduction to optical observation instruments, observational methods, detectors, and data processing.

- **Period**: 1, 2
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, 6 help session for exercises
- **Themes**: Theory 5%, Methods 10%, Observations 75%, Phenomena 10%
- **Teachers**: [[Lauri Jetsu]], [[Thomas Hackman]]

---
## Content

- History
- Atmospheric impact on observations
- Optics
- Telescopes and Observations
- Detectors
- CCD-camera
- Image processing
- Photometry
- Polarimetry
- Spectroscopy
- Astrometry
- Modern telescope technology
