---
title: Radiative Transfer (PAP320)
---

Macroscopic radiative transport, common approximations, and radiative-transfer programs.

- **Period**: 1
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 4 exercises, report work, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 30%, Methods 50%, Observations 10%, Phenomena 10%
- **Teachers**: [[Mika Juvela]]

---
## Content

- Radiative transfer equation
- Setup of radiative transfer models
- Basic radiative transfer methods (ray tracing and Monte Carlo)
- Continuum radiative transfer
- Dust physics and temperature calculation, stochastic heating
- Improvements of the Monte Carlo method
- Line radiative transfer
- Approximate solutions
- Exact solutions with ray tracing and Monte Carlo
- Radiative transfer in multiphysics models
- Model discretisations (spatial, angular, and frequency grids)
- Parallel radiative transfer
