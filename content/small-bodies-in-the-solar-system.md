---
title: Small Bodies in the Solar System (PAP311)
---

Survey of small solar system bodies, including asteroids, comets, and their evolution.

- **Period**: 1, 2
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 13 help sessions, 13 learning logs, 13 self-assessments, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 20%, Methods 10%, Observations 30%, Phenomena 40%
- **Teachers**: [[Mikael Granvik]], [[Eric MacLennan]]

---
## Content

- Asteroid surveys and populations
- Asteroid astrometry and orbits
- Asteroid photometry and rotations
- Asteroid shapes
- Asteroid spectroscopy and surface composition
- Yarkovsky and YORP effects
- Thermal-infrared observations and thermophysical modeling
- Asteroid systems
- Asteroid families
- Active asteroids and meteors
- Asteroid formation, and dynamical and collisional evolution
- Transneptunian objects and Oort-cloud objects
- Centaurs and comets
