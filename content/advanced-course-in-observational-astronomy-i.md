---
title: Advanced Course in Observational Astronomy I (PAP306)
---

Preparation, conduction, and data reduction of modern optical astronomical observations.

- **Period**: 1
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 20 hours observing during intense week, individual report
- **Themes**: Not specified
- **Teachers**: [[Alexis Finoguenov]]

---
## Content

- Principles of optical imaging and spectroscopy
- Telescopes: mounts, hardware and operations
- Nordic Optical Telescope and NOT fellows
- NOT Telescope Control System and its commands
- ALFOSC/NOT operation manual
- Preparation for the observations and observing tools
- Project introduction and target preparation, finding chats and upload
- Project science and its requirements on observations and data reduction
- Observing guide and tips
- Remote observing tools and preparation of NOT Observational Blocks
- NOTCam and its commands
- Preparation for observations at Turku
