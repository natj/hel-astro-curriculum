title: Astrofysiikan peruskurssi II (FYS2047)
---

Interstellar matter physics, spectral line formation, and continuum emission and absorption.

- **Period**: 4
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 6 exercises, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 50%, Methods 20%, Observations 10%, Phenomena 20%
- **Teachers**: [[Mika Juvela]]

---
## Content

- Extinction, absorption by classic dipole
- Scattering by classic dipole
- Continuum absorption (dipole, Hydrogen, other elements)
- Hydrogen ionisation and recombinations
- Transition probabilities
- Spectral line emission and absorption; spectral line profiles
- Radiative transfer equation
- Neutral hydrogen in interstellar space
- Continuum emission and absorption in interstellar space

---
## Pre-requisites

- [[Astrofysiikan peruskurssi I]]
- basic courses in astronomy
- basic courses in physics
- basic studies in theoretical physics or corresponding mathematical skills
