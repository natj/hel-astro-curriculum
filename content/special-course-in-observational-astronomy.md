---
title: Special Course in Observational Astronomy (PAP308)
---

Practical data reduction techniques for modern astronomical data.

- **Period**: 1, 2
- **Content**: 24 lectures, 24 hours of practical work, presentation
- **Themes**: Not specified
- **Teachers**: [[Alexis Finoguenov]], [[Clif Kirkpatrick]]

---
## Content

- IRAF installation
- Optical imaging reduction
- Bias and bias subtraction
- Sensitivity and using sky/dome flats
- Optical spectral reduction
- Basic processing steps
- Tracing the spectrum
- Identification of the spectral features and redshift measurement
- NIR image reduction and spectroscopy
- Software servers at Turku and remote data analysis
- Preparation for observations
- Calibrations during the night and data retrieval
- NOT data processing

---
## Pre-requisites


- Fundamental and Basic Observational Astronomy courses
