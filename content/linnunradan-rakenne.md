title: Linnunradan rakenne (FYS2053)
---

Structure, kinematics, and dynamics of the Milky Way galaxy and its interstellar medium.

- **Period**: 1, 2
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, 7 problem set help sessions, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 45%, Methods 10%, Observations 40%, Phenomena 5%
- **Teachers**: [[Peter Johansson]], [[Jorma Harju]]

---
## Content

- Introduction to the Milky Way
- Observing the distances and velocities of stars in the Milky Way
- Interstellar dust and extinction
- Stellar statistics
- Structure of the Milky Way: Stellar and gaseous disc, stellar bulge, stellar halo, central supermassive black hole, dark matter halo
- GAIA satellite: Revolutionising the study of the Milky Way. Stellar kinematics in the Milky Way
- Galactic rotation. The Oort constants. The Solar orbit and the orbits of other stars in the Milky Way
- The Milky Way rotation curve and spiral structure. The mass density in the vicinity of the Sun
- Formation and evolution of the Milky Way

---
## Pre-requisites


- Basic courses in astronomy
- basic courses in physics
- basic studies in theoretical physics or corresponding mathematical skills
