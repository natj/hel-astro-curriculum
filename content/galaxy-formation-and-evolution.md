---
title: Galaxy formation and evolution (PAP318)
---

Galaxy formation and evolution in a cosmological context.

- **Period**: 1, 2
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, 7 problem set help sessions, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 70%, Methods 10%, Observations 15%, Phenomena 5%
- **Teachers**: [[Peter Johansson]]

---
## Content

- Introduction to galaxy formation and overview
- Observations of galaxies
- Cosmology overview
- Linear evolution of small density perturbations
- Jeans instabilities and horizons in an expanding Universe
- Baryonic and dark matter models of galaxy formation
- Correlation functions and the spectrum of the initial fluctuations
- Non-linear evolution of dark matter haloes
- Formation and evolution of gaseous haloes
- Star formation and stellar feedback in galaxies
- Formation of disk galaxies
- Galaxy interactions and transformations
- Formation of elliptical galaxies
- Formation of active galaxies and the physics of supermassive black holes
