Physics, chemistry, and observation of the interstellar medium and star formation.

- **Period**: 2
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 4 exercises, report work, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 30%, Methods 20%, Observations 20%, Phenomena 30%
- **Teachers**: [[Mika Juvela]]

## Content

- Observations of the interstellar medium
- Stability and structure of interstellar clouds
- Collapse of cloud cores and protostellar evolution
- High-mass star formation
- Astrochemistry
