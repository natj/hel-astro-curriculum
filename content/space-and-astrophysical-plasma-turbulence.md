---
title: Space and Astrophysical Plasma Turbulence (PAP354)
---

Theory and phenomena of plasma turbulence in space and astrophysics contexts.

- **Period**: 2
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 3 exercises, 2 computer exercises, seminar, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 70%, Methods 10%, Observations 10%, Phenomena 10%
- **Teachers**: [[Simon Good]], [[Joonas Nättilä]]

---
## Content

- MHD equations
- Hydrodynamical (Kolmogorov) turbulence
- Weak wave turbulence
- Strong MHD Turbulence
- Intermittency
- Fermi-II particle acceleration mechanism
