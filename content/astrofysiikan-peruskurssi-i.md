Radiative processes, definitions, stellar atmosphere physics, and radiative transfer.

- **Period**: 3
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, 7 problem set help sessions, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 75%, Methods 10%, Observations 5%, Phenomena 10%
- **Teachers**: [[Joonas Nättilä]]

---
## Content

- Brief introduction to the history of astrophysics
- Radiative processes, definitions
- Black-body radiation
- Emission and absorption coefficients
- Radiative transfer, solutions to basic radiative transfer problems
- Solar limb darkening
- Gas physics in the solar atmosphere
- Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution
- Boltzmann equation for atomic excitation
- Saha equation for ionisation
- Thermodynamic equilibrium
- Stellar atmospheric model calculations

---
## Pre-requisites


- Basic courses in astronomy
- basic courses in physics
- basic studies in theoretical physics or corresponding mathematical skills
