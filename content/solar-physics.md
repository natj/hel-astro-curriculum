---
title: Solar Physics (PAP321)
---

Physics, structure, activity cycle, and evolution of the Sun.

- **Period**: 3
- **Content**: 12 lectures, 3 exercises, student seminar
- **Themes**: Theory 40%, Methods 20%, Observations 0%, Phenomena 40%
- **Teachers**: [[Ranadeep Sarkar]], [[Eleanna Asvestari]], [[Simon Good]]

---
## Content

- Formation and evolution of the Sun and Solar System
- Standard solar model
- Solar atmosphere
- Solar oscillations and helioseismology
- Convection and solar rotation
- Solar dynamo and solar cycle
- Solar wind
- Solar eruptions

---
## Pre-requisites

- [[Elektrodynamiikka I]]
- [[Elektrodynamiikka II]]
- [[Mekaniikka]]
- Calculus
- linear algebra
- knowledge of mechanics and electromagnetism
