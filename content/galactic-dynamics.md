---
title: Galactic dynamics (PAP317)
---

Study of galactic dynamics, potential theory, orbits, and Boltzmann equations.

- **Period**: 3, 4
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, 7 problem set help sessions, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 75%, Methods 15%, Observations 5%, Phenomena 5%
- **Teachers**: [[Peter Johansson]]

---
## Content

- Introduction to galactic dynamics
- Potential theory
- The Milky Way potential and Poisson solvers
- Orbits in static spherical potentials
- Orbits in axisymmetric and non-axisymmetric potentials
- Numerical orbit integration
- The collisionless Boltzmann equation
- Distribution functions for spherical isotropic and anisotropic systems
- Jeans and virial equations
- Stellar kinematics as a mass detector: Black holes and dark matter
- Choice of equilibrium and chaotic dynamics
- Stability of collisionless systems
- Disk dynamics and spiral structure
- Kinetic theory and relaxation processes
- Mergers of galaxies and galaxy formation
