## Content

- Conservation laws: continuity equation, Poynting’s theorem, Maxwell’s stress tensor
- Electromagnetic waves: EM waves in vacuum, EM waves in matter, dispersion and absorption, guided waves
- Potentials and fields: potential formulation, retarded potentials, Jefimenko, Liénard-Wiechert, moving charges
- Radiation: Electric and magnetic dipole radiation, arbitrary sources, radiation reaction
- Electrodynamics and relativity: Special relativity, field tensor, Lorentz transformation
