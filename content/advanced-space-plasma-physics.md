---
title: Advanced Space Plasma Physics (PAP323)
---

Advanced theoretical concepts and phenomena in space plasma physics, including reconnection and instabilities.

- **Period**: 3, 4
- **Content**: 24 lectures, 12 exercises
- **Themes**: Theory 70%, Methods 10%, Observations 0%, Phenomena 20%
- **Teachers**: [[Urs Ganse]]

---
## Content

- Detailed particle motion in EM fields, current sheets
- Galactic Cosmic Rays and Solar Energetic Particles
- Wave propagation in dielectric and stratified media
- Vlasov theory and Landau damping
- MHD (force-free fields and flux ropes)
- Plasma instabilities
- Collisionless shock waves
- Magnetic reconnection
- Solar dynamo
- Radiation and scattering
- Transport (Fokker-Planck theory)
