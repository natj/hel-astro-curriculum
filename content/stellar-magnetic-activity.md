---
title: Stellar magnetic activity (PAP351)
---

Physical processes, modelling, and observations of stellar magnetic activity.

- **Period**: 3, 4
- **Content**: 14 lectures, 6 exercises, exam
- **Themes**: Theory 30%, Methods 20%, Observations 20%, Phenomena 30%
- **Teachers**: [[Thomas Hackman]]

---
## Content

- Basic MHD concepts
- Solar and stellar dynamos
- Numerical dynamo simulations
- Manifestations of solar and stellar magnetic activity
- Solar/stellar corona and winds
- Star-planet magnetic interaction
- Observing solar and stellar magnetic activity
- Inversion methods (Doppler imaging, photometric inversion)
