---
title: Space Applications of Plasma Physics (PAP305)
---

Application of plasma physics to space environments, solar wind, and magnetospheres.

- **Period**: 2
- **Content**: 12 lectures, practical hands-on exercises
- **Themes**: Theory 20%, Methods 70%, Observations 0%, Phenomena 10%
- **Teachers**: [[Eleanna Asvestari]], [[Lucile Turc]]

---
## Content

- Basics of the Sun, its atmosphere
- Solar wind and interplanetary shocks
- Earth’s magnetosphere and ionosphere
- Magnetospheric storms and substorms
- Space weather
- Key data sets in space physics
