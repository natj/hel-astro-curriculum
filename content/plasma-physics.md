Fundamental properties of plasma, single particle motion, kinetic theory, and magnetohydrodynamics.

- **Period**: 1
- **Content**: 13 lectures, 6 exercises
- **Themes**: Theory 70%, Methods 0%, Observations 10%, Phenomena 20%
- **Teachers**: [[Minna Palmroth]]

## Content

- Basic plasma parameters
- Single particle motion
- Kinetic plasma description
- Macroscopic plasma description
- MHD waves
- Cold plasma waves

- Basic physics courses
- Solid calculation skills
